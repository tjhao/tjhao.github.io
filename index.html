About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate supervised by Prof. Bruno C. d. S. Oliveira at the University of Hong Kong (HKU). My research interests include programming languages, formal verification, theorem proving, and logics. Currently I am working on first-class environments and intersection types, as well as their applications.
