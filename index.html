About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate supervised by Prof. Bruno C. d. S. Oliveira at the University of Hong Kong (HKU). My research is centered around programming languages. In particular, my research interests include

  • semantics: environment-based semantics, abstract machines, program transformations

  • type systems: (disjoint) intersection types, subtyping, parametricity

  • formal verification: mechanized formalization (in Coq), SMT solving
